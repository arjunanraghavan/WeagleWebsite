ABOUT US

Who We Are

WEAGLE comes with a vision for Innovation, Excellence, Professionalism and Sustainability. WEAGLE is known for building long-lasting value for its customers. The leadership at WEAGLE persistently strives towards delivering performance that makes a difference. With over decade of experience in Real Estate, coupled with a commitment to provide our customers with the best in class design and quality, WEAGLE today has completed 50+ projects in the city and remains dedicated in delivering landmark projects, and building trusting relationships with all our clients.

What We Do

We are specialized in building and selling luxurious and completely furnished villas all over the state. We undertake constructions of any residential, commercial buildings and the layout works to complex double basement developments to a new building.

Our Mission

Weagle is forging ahead to bring about the highest quality into their various high-end projects. A Weagle construction's home owner is one who looks for highest standard in construction practices, the use of superior building products and customer satisfaction at every aspect of business. We are committed to building beautiful and innovative homes, all the while by providing exceptional customer service with trust, transparency, and fairness.
